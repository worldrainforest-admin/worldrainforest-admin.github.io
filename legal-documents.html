Tax Deductibility

As a 501(c)(3) organization, contributions to the World Rainforest Fund are tax-deductible for US taxpayers. Donors will receive a receipt for their records that can be used when filing income tax returns.

The deductibility of charitable contributions is subject to certain limitations based on income and other factors. Donors should consult with their tax advisors regarding the deductibility of their specific contributions.

Financial Transparency

The World Rainforest Fund is committed to the highest standards of financial transparency and accountability. We maintain detailed financial records and comply with all applicable federal and state regulations.

Our annual financial reports and Form 990 tax returns are available to donors and the public upon request. We believe donors have the right to know how their contributions are used and the impact of their support.
